----------------------------------------------------------------
SOCIAL MEDIA DO'S AND DON'TS CHECKLIST
(For College Students)
----------------------------------------------------------------
 
DO'S ✔
-------
1. Keep your LinkedIn and GitHub profiles professional, updated,
   and complete with your academic and project details.
 
2. Think before you post — ask yourself if the content is
   something you would be comfortable showing a future employer.
 
3. Use strong, unique passwords for every social media account
   and enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
 
4. Give credit when sharing others' work — always tag or mention
   the original creator.
 
5. Use social media to network positively — connect with peers,
   professors, and professionals in your field of interest.
 
6. Report any abusive, harassing, or suspicious content/accounts
   using the platform's built-in reporting tools.
 
7. Keep your privacy settings reviewed and updated regularly to
   control who can see your posts and personal information.
 
 
DON'TS ✘
---------
1. Do not share personal details like your phone number, home
   address, Aadhaar number, or bank details on any public platform.
 
2. Do not post negative, offensive, or controversial content about
   classmates, teachers, or institutions — it can have serious
   academic and professional consequences.
 
3. Do not click on unknown links or pop-ups received via DMs,
   comments, or emails — these are often phishing attempts.
 
4. Do not use social media accounts belonging to others or create
   fake profiles — this is unethical and potentially illegal.
 
5. Do not share exam papers, confidential college documents, or
   copyrighted study material online without permission.
 
6. Do not engage with or respond to cyberbullies — block and
   report immediately and inform a trusted adult or authority.
 
7. Do not accept friend/follow requests from strangers without
   verifying their identity, especially on personal platforms.
