## 📌 Project Overview

This repository is my Digital Literacy Portfolio submitted as part of
the CSE0001 course at VIT Bhopal University. The project is divided
into five tasks, each mapped to a module of the course. As a Student
Digital Ambassador, I have documented my learning across digital tools,
professional online presence, coding platforms, email etiquette, and
cybercrime awareness.

## 📝 Module Summaries

### ✅ Task 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic (Module 1)
Created a one-page digital literacy infographic using Canva covering
the topics of what digital literacy is, safe internet practices, useful
digital tools for students, and professional online presence. The
infographic is designed to help fellow students understand the
importance of being digitally literate in today's world.

### ✅ Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio (Module 2)
Set up professional profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. Created
a GitHub profile README with personal and academic details. Updated
the LinkedIn Education section with current degree information. These
platforms will serve as the foundation of my professional digital
identity over the next four years.

### ✅ Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms (Module 3)
Created an account on HackerRank and completed beginner-level Python
challenges including "Say Hello, World! With Python". Also built a
5-question Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz using Google Forms with
multiple choice and short answer questions for my batchmates.

### ✅ Task 4 – Professional Email & Etiquette Guide (Module 4)
Drafted two professional emails — one requesting a deadline extension
from a professor, and one expressing interest in a summer internship
to a company coordinator. Also created a Social Media Do's and Don'ts
checklist with 7 points each for responsible use as a college student.

### ✅ Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness Case Study (Module 5)
Researched and wrote a detailed case study on UPI/Online Payment Fraud,
one of the most common cybercrimes targeting college students in India.
Created a comprehensive "Stay Safe Online" prevention checklist with
12 actionable tips including UPI-specific safety advice and emergency
reporting contacts.
